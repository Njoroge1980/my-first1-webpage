MY FAVORITE PLACES

I've been rather fortunate to be able to visit a few places locally and beyond during my work. The choice of places below is of course based on these experiences with probably one fantasy destination i think i might one day want to visit

Bangkok

The capital city of Thailand is a hot and humid concrete jungle. I find it beautiful, but likely because i seem to have had idylic layovers there. I like it so much that i had my honeymoon there too. The traffic can be horendous but its easy to get around on foot or by the electric train. Places i like visiting include the markets Klong Thom, Sampeng, Bobay etc. I'm not much of a night scene person but Khaosan road and Patpong are there for those that like that kind of revelry
